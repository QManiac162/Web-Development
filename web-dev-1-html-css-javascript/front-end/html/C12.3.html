Dragon Ball Z

By Deep Samanta

The Dragon Ball Z continues the adventures of Goku in his adult life as he and his companions defend the Earth against villains including aliens (Vegeta, Frieza), androids (Cell), and magical creatures (Majin Buu). At the same time, the story parallels the life of his son, Gohan, as well as the development of his rivals, Piccolo and Vegeta.

Characters:

  • Vegeta
  • Frieza
  • Majin Buu
  • Gohan
  • Piccolo

Vegeta is the prince of an extraterrestrial warrior race known as the Saiyans

Frieza is widely considered to be the most iconic villain from the Dragon Ball franchise

Majin Buu is a genie-like magical life form awoken by the evil warlock Bibidi

Gohan is initially trained by his father's former nemesis Piccolo, ultimately becoming one of the strongest

Piccolo is a member of an extraterrestrial humanoid species called Namekians
